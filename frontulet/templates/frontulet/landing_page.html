Meet Erulet

Erulet is a figure from Aranese mythology. He is a gnome who lives hidden in the forests, representing the spirit of nature. And he comes to you now . . . in an app.

Start your adventure

Discover the waters of the Val d'Aran

Erulet will guide you through a series of adventures, along rivers and around mountain lakes. He will show you the flora and fauna of the Val d'Aran, and teach you about the valley's aquatic ecosystems.

Learn more

Hike with a new kind of trail guide

The Erulet app is a trail guide unlike anything you've used before. It will track your progress and notify you of points of interest along the way. Enjoy interactive, multimedia content created by scientists who have been working in these mountains for years to understand their unique ecosystems. Mark the routes with your own notes, photos, and videos, and share them with fellow hikers.
